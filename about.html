About Sean

Sean is a dedicated and experienced psychiatric nurse practitioner with a passion for providing compassionate and comprehensive mental health care. He holds a Master of Science in Nursing (MSN) degree and is board certified as a Psychiatric-Mental Health Nurse Practitioner (PMHNP-BC).

With over 10 years of experience in the mental health field, Sean has worked with diverse populations, helping clients navigate various mental health challenges. His areas of expertise include mood disorders, anxiety disorders, and trauma-related disorders.

Sean believes in a holistic approach to mental health care, incorporating evidence-based practices, medication management, and therapeutic interventions tailored to each client's unique needs. He is committed to partnering with his clients on their mental health journey, fostering an environment of trust, understanding, and growth.
